Death school

About death wizards

Being a death wizard can be difficult...

If you don't have insane damage and crit-chance, you won't really be able to do anything

The only thing you have going for yourself are spells that heal you when you deal damage

Which means you are only prolonging your inevitable death by attacking

How to possibly win as a death wizard

What to bring to the duel:

  • Blades
  • Traps
  • Pack some shields
  • Lulu

    • Strat:

    • Start blading and trapping heavily
    • Shield to not die if the opponent is gonna attack
    • Hope for a shadow pip and lulu for that sweet damage and heal
    • Repeat
